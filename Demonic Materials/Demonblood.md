Also referred to as Unholy Water, Ichor or in scientific literature Demolymph, is, put most succinctly, liquidised demon. Whenever the flesh of a Demon is exposed it secretes Demonblood from the site of the wound. It does this regardless of its state of desiccation and regardless of how much effort has previously been undertaken to induce hypohemia, a lack of blood. In this process the demon loses mass equivalent to the mass of the blood excreted.
For reasons that will become apparent in the following points, it is hypothesised that demons produce [[Demonblood]] by rapidly liquifying their own tissue. 

# Demonblood Phenomena
While some of the properties of Demonblood are equivalent to those known of mammalian blood, it also displays some unusual properties.

Most pressing among these is [[Sanguilegious Contact]], which allows Demonblood to assimilate any sanguine liquid. Secondarily however [[Demogulation]] which allows collections of [[Demonblood]] to coagulate into living demonic material.
