For one, Demons at the Stalingrad Incursion Site have left behind Exoskeletons packed with dust. In the early days of the Incursion said dust was described as of the same properties as Pure [[Demonbone]], later however, it became observed as mudlike.
Another puzzling case is that of the Demons utilized in Normandy. This incursion produced Demons which displayed no internal skeleton at all relying fully on their external skeleton for structural support. None such Demons have been produced since the Normandy incursion has been stabilized and their inert Skeletons remain the only proof of their existence.
