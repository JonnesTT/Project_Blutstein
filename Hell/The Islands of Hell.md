There is however, one more role filled by Sanguinite as found naturally within the [[Hell|P.o.P.]] As our excursions have shown repeatedly, the floating islands of the Plane are in large parts made up of [[Demonbone]] sparsely filled in with [[Fleshgrowths]]. That includes, yes, the uncountably many bones that demons have left behind. More vexing however are the large plateaus and seemingly earthlike mountains. While the plateaus particularly are often compacted in a manner similar to earthlike sediment, many mountains reveal hollow, spongiform interiors much in the manner in which bones are left behind.
