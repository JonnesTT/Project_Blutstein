Within [[Hell]] texts are imbued into [[Puddles of Will]]. These are drawn by blood into the underlying surface at the will of one who's blood is spilt.

# Appearance
[[Puddles of Will]] flow forth evenly from [[Demons]], thus they are most commonly approximately circular. Their shapes are marked by channels and collection points, which may be misunderstood to be the symbol in itself. 

# Deciphering 
One may see the distinct lines of [[Puddles of Will]] and conclude that they are a form of writing. One would be wrong in that assumption. 
While some meaning can be gleamed from the relation between shapes and channels within [[Puddles of Will]], the contextual mass would remain lost. According to documents pertaining to [[The Wiederkehrer]] reconstructed from the ashes of various facilities of the Ahnenerbe, [[Puddles of Will]] are fully and forthrightly a direct storage of limited but contextual experience. Unfortunately for humankind, the medium of encoding is living [[Demonblood]] and thus any direct contact would cause infection.
