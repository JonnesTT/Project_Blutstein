# Likely Locations
Spawning Pits are typically found in Demon Fortresses as their content is considered valuable among demons. They may however appear near battlefields as well, allowing factions downhill to utilise or harvest the expended blood. There they are usually defended and entrenched with palisades made from bones of those dismantled in it's fueling.

These battlefield pits are also commonly found on earth near past Incursion sites. These act as outposts, spawning large demons primarily to transport blood pilfered in this less hostile environment.

# Tactical Implications
Spawning Pits are a mode of reinforcement, it is of utmost necessity that any and all Spawning Pits near potential combat sites are destroyed. 
Those Pits used as logistical hubs may be less relevant to combat effectiveness, they do however exert a constant drain on both their local civilians population as well as the morale of nearby troops. Therefore it is recommended that Environmental Pits are destroyed when safely possible.
