Sanguilegious Contact also referred to as Desecration of Blood or, most formally, the assimilative property of demonic material, is the property of demonic material to assimilate any Sanguineous liquid including but not limited to Mammalian blood, fish blood, the blood of octopodes and horseshoe crabs, the hemolymph of insects and the plasma of single celled organisms.
During this process, the donor blood takes on the consistency and colour of Demonblood as well as any of its unusual properties. Thus it becomes impossible to differentiate between donor and demonic blood after the process is complete.
