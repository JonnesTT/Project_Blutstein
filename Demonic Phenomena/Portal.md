Portals are localised phenomena in which a physical direction points towards another plane of existence. This allows people and material to move into another plane of existence without being natively aware or capable of 4th dimensional movement.

Portals are used by [[Demons]] to breach into our plane of existence.
# Mechanics
The construction of portals is not known, nor are the fundamental mechanics of portal construction only few phenomena have been observed.
## Directionality 
From observing the directionality of light at the edge of portals, we can infer that they bend the direction of space towards [[Hell]] much like how gravity bends the direction of time towards super heavy objects. 
One way portals may indeed involve [[Gravity Leaking]], as they transport those who stand inside the designated space gradually into [[Hell]]. 
Thus providing interdimensional distance per time until one "collides" with an interdimensional barrier in hell.
Two way portals are likely to function in another way, as they provide interdimensional distance per distance traveled inside the portal hallway.
