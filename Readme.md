# Content Warning 
Project Blutstein is a world-building project inspired by the 2016 reboot of the Doom franchise as well ss the Wolfenstein Reboot.
As such it focuses on modifications to mundane reality based on blood, gore and bodyhorror. These will be presented earnest but comically throughout the project.
That cannot be said for the secondary focus of this project, representing the absolute depth of the depravity of Nazism. To that end topics of human experimentation, dehumanisation, human sacrifice and genocide will be presented with an absolute focus and lacking diversions of any kind. The resulting depictions are intended to be upsetting and will disturb some readers. 
It is my hope that this depiction portrays the horrors of Nazi germany in it's fullest extend, as I do not believe that these horrors can be heightened into the cinematic super-realism of much modern fiction.

# The Projects Philosophy
Project Blutstein is not the main Project I work on and it is not the project in which I stake my self image as writer. Thus it may lie dormant for months ln end or temporarily gain texts with striking grammatical errors. 
However, this lack of care also allows me to experiment with Creative Commons IP and Community Contribution. So from the initial release on the 08.04.26, onwards, this entire project is availible under a CC BY-SA 4.0 license.

If you enjoy anything relating to Project Blutstein, you have the right to reproduce or adapt it into any medium on any platform without requiring any permission to do so. Want to Paint something described here? You can! Want to print it in your zeen? You can! You can even fork the entire repo and setup shop if my iron fisted administration doesn't suit you!

You must however extend the same grace to those who will come after you. Anything you create as part of Project Blutstein must be open for anyone to use for any purpose. Others may use your images in their YouTube videos, or print your texts in their zeenes. But it also means that I may add your creations to this repo and make it an official part of Project Blutstein at any time and without asking you.

However, this license includes an attribution requirement as described in the 4.0 Creative Commons Catalogue.
