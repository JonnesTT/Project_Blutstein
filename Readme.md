# Content Warning 
Project Blutstein focuses on modifications to mundane reality based on **blood, gore and body horror as well as the harms of Nazism**.
While I aim to represent body horror with a certain whimsy, I do not aim make light of any of the crimes of the Nazis. I am committed to representing the full depth of the depravity of Nazism earnestly and without deemphasizing the harm done in any way. It is thus that **the depictions of Nazi crimes within this project are meant to be upsetting**.
It is my hope that this depiction portrays the horrors of Nazi Germany in absolute earnestness, as I believe that any deviation from earnestness would be an insult to the victims of the Nazi project.

**Proceed into the projects documentation at your own risk.**

# The Projects Philosophy
Project Blutstein is not my main writing Project. And it is not the project in which I stake my self-image as writer. Thus, it may lie dormant for months on end or temporarily gain texts with striking grammatical errors. 
However, this lack of care also allows me to experiment with Creative Commons IP and Community Contribution. So from the initial release on the 08.04.26, onwards, this entire project is available under a CC BY-SA 4.0 license.

If you enjoy anything relating to Project Blutstein, you have the right to reproduce or adapt it into any medium on any platform without requiring any permission to do so. Want to Paint something described here? You can! Want to print it in your zeen? You can! You can even fork the entire repo and setup shop if my iron-fisted administration doesn't suit you!

You must however extend the same grace to those who will come after you. Anything you create as part of Project Blutstein must be open for anyone to use for any purpose. Others may use your images in their YouTube videos, or print your texts in their zeenes. But it also means that I may add your creations to this repo and make it an official part of Project Blutstein at any time and without asking you.

However, this license includes an attribution requirement as described in the 4.0 Creative Commons Catalogue.
