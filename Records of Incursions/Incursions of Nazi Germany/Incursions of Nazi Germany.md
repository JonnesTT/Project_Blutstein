The existence of [[Demons]] has been recorded in myth since myth itself. Before the [[Verdun Incursion of 1940]] however, the occurrence of Demons was almost universally theorised to be purely metaphorical. Unfortunately, we these theories are wrong.

It is theorised that demonic interest was directed on the Nazi projekt as a direct result of the mass killing of cultural minorities, physically disabled people, mentally disabled people, any perceived threat to their social identity, the ideologically unaligned, artists producing art which did not glorify the Nazi project, those whose land or positions the Nazis wanted to take for themselves as well as the soldiers they sent to die in pursuit of their project.
As all documents pertaining to Demonic Research and [[Fremdblut Strategy]] have been destroyed and no communicative contact with Demons established, the precise sequence of events could not be determined.
What is known is that the frequency and amount of killing has increased starkly after the Juli of 1936 and that the manner of killing changed significantly with the introduction of [[Human Sacrifices at Concentration Camps]].

It is as of yet unknown hoe the Nazis opened the incursion portals used in the [[Fremdblut Strategy]]. Their active planning of this doctrine however implies that they had significant control. Additionally, the overt auto-desangionation displayed by the soldiers enacting both the [[Verdun Incursion of 1940]] and [[The Stalingrad Incursion]] implies active participation by them and some utility of their death or the release of blood.
