Many soldiers were exposed to [[Demonblood]] during the execution of [[Blut des Blutes Strategem]]. Most of them were lost forever, turning into demonic beasts before being "recycled". Thanks to the psudoreligious indoctrination of the SS, and the emphasis of service and self sacrifice within this cult however some were able to force their own will upon the blood within.
The physical manifestation of the new demonic self had likely already begun at this point as all Wiederkehrer sighted are reported to have had similar, notably demonic physical features.

# Appearance
All sighted Wiederkehrer are reported to have thin, skin that seems stretched over their gaunt features. Their hair is said to have fallen out of being spotty at best, while their teeth have grown significantly in both length and sharpness.
Reports also indicate that [[The Wiederkehrer]] possess some kind of claws. Although perhaps one report put it most expressively: "The tip of their fingers lacked nails. nails and skin and flesh. Like they had clawed themselves out of their own coffins. That bit (the outermost digit) was longer too than in should be, don't ask me how I know, it just looks wrong."

# Capabilities
Not much of the research the Nazis did on [[The Wiederkehrer]] could be recovered. However a high level report captured on its way to Himmler portrays their capabilities as living weapons quite severely. Please be aware that the following is likely embellished to court favour.

Translation:
Physiologically, the afflicted show considerable explosive strength implying a mostly demonic musculature. While we do not have records before their affliction, we can confidently say that their strength on this metric has likely at least doubled.
Unfortunately, this increase comes at the cost of endurance strength. While their endurance seems practically unlimited, their strength under load has suffered greatly. Measurements imply a reduction of approximately 30-50% against the average.
Their weakness however start in the mental realm. At a glance, their intellectual abilities seem to be unaffected. Speech is still possible, they display an ability to navigate spaces beyond expectation and their reasoning seems normal at first.
When tested in detail however, their responses to complex problems are hollow. As if they don't fully understand the subject matter, and merely recount known answers.
Indeed, when confronted with aptitude tests which they are unlikely to have been confronted before, they got frustrated almost immediately eventually culminating in violence. 
Their psychological metrics corroborate a near nonexistent frustration threshhold. More worrying still is the detachment with which they view others now. Almost all of the personel we lost during testing was partially consumed by the time we could retrieve their bodies. Not one of the perpetrators showed any sign of regret for this.

It is my opinion, and that of my entire staff that these soldiers are no longer arian. Much more their will to consume other creatures and enhanced speed make them actively dangerous to any living being in their vicinity.
While I cannot rule out a possibility for treatment, I must recommend that any afflicted are liquidated before they stain their hands with arian blood.
Should my recommendation be rejected, I believe that the [redacted] Kurheim is best suited to research treatment thanks to our experience with the afflicted. The nearby [redacted] barracks would also offer an interesting chance to assess their altitude in military matters.

Heil
Dr. Wegner
